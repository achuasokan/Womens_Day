Mary Kom

Six-time World Boxing Champion, Olympic Medalist & Inspiration to Millions

Biography

Mangte Chungneijang Mary Kom, popularly known as Mary Kom, is an Indian Olympic boxer from Manipur. She has made history as the only woman to win the World Amateur Boxing Championship six times. Despite facing numerous obstacles, her perseverance, dedication, and passion for boxing have made her an inspiration to millions.
