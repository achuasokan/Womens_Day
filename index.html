Biography

The Magnificent Mary

Mangte Chungneijang Mary Kom, popularly known as Mary Kom, is an Indian Olympic boxer from Manipur. Born on March 1, 1983, she has made history as the only woman to win the World Amateur Boxing Championship six times.

Despite facing numerous obstacles, her perseverance, dedication, and passion for boxing have made her an inspiration to millions. Her journey from a small village in Manipur to becoming a global boxing icon is truly remarkable.
